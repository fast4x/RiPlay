<h2>🎵 About RiPlay</h2>

RiPlay is a powerful, open-source music player designed for music lovers who want complete control over their listening experience. With support for both online and offline content, extensive customization options, and a beautiful interface, RiPlay transforms how you interact with your music collection.

<hr>

<h2>⚠️ Important Notice</h2>

<h3>🛡️ RiPlay respects your privacy and operates transparently</h3>
<ul>
  <li>No data collection, your data stays on your device</li>
  <li>No download or caching online content</li>
  <li>For playing online content, it uses the YouTube Player library as a wrapper around the official [IFrame Player API](https://developers.google.com/youtube/iframe_api_reference) inside a WebView</li>
</ul>

<hr>

<h2>✨ Key Features</h2>

<h3>🎧 Music Experience</h3>
<ul>
<li>Play songs, videos, artists, albums, playlists, and podcasts</li>
<li>Smart queues (audio/video mixed)</li>
<li>Listening statistics</li>
<li>Lyrics support: fetch, display, edit, translate</li>
<li>Listener level ranking with personal monthly and annual badge</li>
<li>Blacklist, can ignore artist, album, Song, Playlist, Device folder</li>
<li>Rewind, your year in music</li>
</ul>

<h3>⏰ Smart Features</h3>
<ul>
<li>Music Recognize (title & artist)</li>
<li>Sleep timer</li>
<li>Visualizer (microphone permission required)</li>
<li>Fast Share (direct sharing with installed apps)</li>
<li>Cast to another Android device / TV via [RiLink](https://github.com/fast4x/RiLink) (experimental)</li>
<li>Self-closing app scheduling</li>
<li>Events daily/weekly, new releases from your artists, auto backup and check update</li>
<li>Android Auto support</li>
<li>Android TV support (experimental)</li>
<li>Widget support (experimental)</li>
</ul>
